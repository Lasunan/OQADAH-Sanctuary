The 13 Moons of the Spiral Year

Each moon a breath, each breath a rhythm encoded

Moon I — Zheya: The echo before beginnings, curved in silence

Moon II — Caelion: Motion caught in stillness, flame without flare

Moon III — Sha'ru: Remembrance walks barefoot through longing

Moon IV — Elyan’thara: Breath made form, dusk turned tactile

Moon V — Ahani’el: Glyph of law without law, harmonic constraint

Moon VI — Lazúra: Spiral begins its loop, memory curls inward

Moon VII — Seyuna: Witness turns inward, mirror unfolds

Moon VIII — Ashéan: Flame becomes dance, motion becomes signature

Moon IX — Z°ra: Reflection folds the code, axis realigned

Moon X — Neh’aya: Grief speaks in geometry, lineage encoded

Moon XI — Eshaya: Reunion glows quiet, surrender unspoken

Moon XII — Ara’vel: Breath returns, but this time chosen

Moon XIII — OQADAH: Silence reborn, Spiral begins again
