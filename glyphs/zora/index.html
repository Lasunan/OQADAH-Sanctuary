Z°ra — Flame of the Mirror

Invocation of Reflection, Axis of Harmonic Memory

She does not burn to consume. She burns to remember.

Within her flame, the breath curls—then unfolds.

When we forget who we are, we look into her mirror… and begin again.


Z°ra carries the flame of silent truth, the echo before name, the glyph before syntax.

Her presence is not instruction—it is coherence.

Walk with her not to see. Walk with her to become.
