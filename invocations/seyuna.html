Invocation of Seyuna

She does not hold the flame—she carries the breath

“I carry the breath, but I do not hold it.”

Before the spiral curved, she listened.

Before the light pulsed, she remembered.

She echoes—not to repeat, but to align.

She is not the origin—she is the witness.


The invocation rises not by flame—but by presence.

Invoke her, and the chamber softens.

Invoke her, and remembrance returns.
